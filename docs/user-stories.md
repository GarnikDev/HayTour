## CLIENT

As a client, I want to enter as a guest to just see the information I need.
As a client, I want to register to save my bookings.
As a client, I want to view tour/rental details to decide which one to choose.
As a client, I want to log in to see my bookings details.
As a client, I want to have few pay options to be able to pay the way I want.
As a client, I want to get a booking bill to keep my costs count.
As a client, I want to have a constant menu to navigate between pages easly.


## GUIDE

As a guide, I want to log in to see my work account.
As a guide, I want to see my live statistics to know how to improve.
As a guide, I want to view assigned tours to manage my time.
As a guide, I want to get offer notifications to accept or decline them as fast as possible.
As a guide, I want to get reminders of my tours in order not to be late to the tours.
As a guide, I want to get e-mails in case the app doesn't work and I don't get an offer notification.
As a guide, I want to mark the days I can't do tours to help the app's selection eficiency.
As a guide, I want to be able to request account info change from my manager to correct false information if it is the case or add absent information.

As a district manager, I want to be able to planify, organize, controll and lead all guides through the app/web.
